The Cappuccino

It's like sipping the perfect mixture of milk and coffee.....because it is the perfect mixture of milk and coffee! Cappuccinos are an italian masterpiece that were created for coffee lovers of most ages. It consists of a simple mixture of espresso, steamed milk, and a dollop of milk foam to top it off. It is the perfect way to start your morning or to continue a long day in a great mood.

Ingredients Required

  • Espresso
  • Whole Milk
  • Any toppings you prefer

Preparation Instructions

  1. Brew a shot or two of espresso into a cappuccino cup
  2. Steam milk using a steam wand just under the surface to create lots of milk foam
  3. Pour steamed milk into espresso creating a beverage that is 1 part coffee 1 part milk.
  4. Ladle a dollop of milk foam on top (or a foam mountain!)
