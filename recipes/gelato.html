Homemade Gelato

Gelato will forever hold a sweet spot in my heart. I love everything about this frozen treat from the rich decadent flavors, all the way to the smooth low milk-fat mouth feel. Gelato is very similar to American Ice Cream - with the exception being that it uses much lower fat-containing milk. It's the perfect treat for a hot summer evening!

Ingredients Required

  • 4 medium egg yolks
  • 150g granulated sugar
  • 350 mL of whole milk
  • 250 mL of heavy cream
  • grated lemon zest or vanilla extract/li>

Preparation Instructions

  1. Whip the egg yolks and sugar at high speed for 5 minuntes - creating soft peaks
  2. Bring the milk to JUST UNDER a boil for 5 minutes (185F)
  3. Transfer the egg and sugar cream to a saucepan and slowly add the heated milk - mixing while pouring. Leave on heat for additional 5 minutes, remove from heat, and then add cold cream.
  4. Add lemon zest or vanilla extract now
  5. Pour the concoction and place into the freezer for about 5 hours. Every 30 minutes break the frozen surface to homogenize the mixture and prevent ice crystal formation.

    6. Buon Appetito!
